Prelude

Since Donpheebin is one of the best metal bands, yet, it still remains unknown to most people, I decided I would try to persuade some to listen to them. This is a tribute to them in which I'll be posting their lyrics, translated into English.
The lyrics have been translated by a real Thai user, into English. I'm solely posting them into this website.
